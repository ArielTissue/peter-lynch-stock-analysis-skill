# Peter Lynch Stock Analysis

Use this skill to analyze stocks through a Peter Lynch-inspired framework.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the investment question: buy, hold, sell, watchlist, comparison, or position sizing.
2. Read `references/peter-lynch-framework.md` before applying the framework.
3. Classify the company using the framework's company categories.
4. Evaluate business quality, growth runway, earnings, valuation, balance sheet, risks, and catalysts.
5. Compare the stock against the investor's portfolio style and risk tolerance.
6. Produce a disciplined conclusion with thesis, watch points, decision triggers, and risks.

## Rules

- Verify current prices, financials, and news with up-to-date sources when the user asks for a current investment view.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and judgment.
- Avoid giving blind buy/sell instructions. Frame conclusions as decision support.
- Highlight what would change the thesis.
